About me

Williams Chukwu is a visiting assistant professor at Wake Forest University in North Carolina United States. I am also a researcher in mathematical model of infectious and non-infectious diseases. With an application of optimal control and machine learning to investigate several mitigation strategies to curb the spread of diseases. My work focuses on the use of ordinary diffrential equation at population level to predict emergence and resurgence of epidemics.
